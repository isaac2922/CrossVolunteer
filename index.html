Organized Volunteering

Definition: an organization that is composed of or functions with the aid of volunteers and which provides aid or services to individuals, groups or countries

Organizing volunteers involves:

Setting goals: Determine what you want to achieve and break it down into smaller tasks.

Calculating needs: Determine how many volunteers you need and what resources they'll need.

Preparing: Make sure resources are ready before volunteers arrive.

Communicating: Provide clear instructions and start on time.

Recognizing: Show appreciation for volunteers

Local Volunteering

Definition : Volunteering can help you meet new people, strengthen your connection to the community, and expose you to people with common interests

Examples : serving at a soup kitchen, help at events, assist schools, donate books, help at library, help at animal shelters, donate at orphanage

Organizations

International Ideas

Definition : International volunteering can provide practical experience that employers value, and it's an opportunity to meet people from all around the world and create professional and personal connections

Examples : teach children around the world, plant trees/protect the environment, donate blood, be an online teacher, host a cleanup, help the homeless internationally, help build homes, schools, hospitals

Organizations

IVPA, Global Volunteers, IVHQ, GoEco, GVI, Kaya Responsible Travel, One World 365, WWOOF, All Hands and Hearts
